Hobbies

Drawing

Drawing is a very soothing (although sometimes frustrating) pastime. I get to admire every part of the subject as I take important attention to detail. I love to see the picture in all its stages from a sketch to a polished version. The greatest part of drawing is that there is always something to improve and the piece is never truly finished.

Photography

I love photography almost as much as I love drawing. Photography is like painting a picture with a lens except now I am capturing all the natural colors and patterns as how they really are as opposed to what I perceive. Photography also allows me to capture great memories and make some awesome videos. Photos also are great complements to projects (like websites).

Adventure

Even more than drawing or photography, I love to travel and spend time with my family. Traveling allows me to be exposed to new experiences; seeing animals and plants I haven’t before, meeting people from all walks of life, and tasting different foods and spices.

Experience

Uncle Ed's BBQ & Catering

Growing up, I have always worked in the family business which was a restaurant called Uncle Ed’s BBQ & Catering until 2012. Although my parents retired out of in-house catering, we are still vendors during the summer at the Racine, Kenosha, and Walworth County fairs in Wisconsin.

Uncle Ed's Watercrafts

Since we still own the Uncle Ed's BBQ and Catering building which resides on Powers Lake, we rent piers and watercrafts such as pontoons & tubes, wave runners, pedalboards, and kayaks. I give our guests tours on the lake and show them how to operate the machinery. I also talk them through our liability forms.

Target

When I am not working with my parents, I am working at Target. I work in the Consumables department. I love working with food and exchanging recipes with customers. I also get exposed to new food products which is always exciting.
