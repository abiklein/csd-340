Profile

The nineteen-year-old, Brandon Moffitt, could be described by others as someone who is persistent, informative, and approachable. He would go on to describe himself as also smart, determined, and technical. Brandon’s and others’ observations of his own characteristics more than reflect the values that he lives by,

""[My life so far is] that of a pretty standard one: do what you are supposed to do, work hard, and good things come… Work for what you want. Don’t let others determine what you are capable of. What seems impossible, is possible from another perspective.” - Brandon Moffitt

Family

Brandon gained these values throughout his life which started in Madison, WI, where he was born. Shortly after his birth, Brandon and his family moved to Joliet, Il and finally to St. Charles, Il where Brandon resides part time with his two sisters, Megan Moffitt (13) and Emma Moffitt (20); his father, Greg Moffitt (49); his mother, Tara Moffitt (43); and his three dogs: Cooper, Rocky, and Smokey. The other part of his time is spent living closer to University of Wisconsin – Parkside where he is currently pursuing a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science. Even though he is away from home, Brandon and his family still maintain a tight relationship,

“We are all very close, though we have the times where we butt heads. Small stuff.” - Brandon Moffitt

Goals

Brandon also has been diagnosed with ADHD since childhood, finding it difficult to conduct redundant tasks. As such, Brandon’s career goal is to avoid an office. He would prefer to work remotely where he can move around. Brandon’s greatest dream is to improve his business, one of his hobbies that he is currently working to make it into a career, enough to support him and his future family.
